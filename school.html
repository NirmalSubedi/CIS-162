Cerritos College

I started attending Cerritos College in the fall of 2021. It was the start of Freshman year in college. I had already heard a lot of things about college being hard. And being the step to been taken to land a high-paying job or to pursue your own career. I had a lot of expectations set up for college, some being from people who previously attended college themselves, while others being from those that didn't emphasize the difficulty, when hearing about the topics and how extensively the classes get taught. However, when I did finally attend college, I found the expectations that I set for myself to be meaningless and overexaggerated.
